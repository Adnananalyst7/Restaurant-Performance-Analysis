Project Summary :

This Tableau dashboard analyzes restaurant performance data across multiple cities to uncover insights related to customer ratings, pricing segmentation, franchise expansion, and customer engagement.

As part of my transition from Quality Assurance Engineering to Data Analytics, this project demonstrates my ability to transform raw data into actionable business insights through data visualization and storytelling.

Business Objectives :

Identify top-performing restaurants based on ratings and votes

2. Analyze price range distribution across cities

3. Determine high-density restaurant hubs

4. Evaluate franchise expansion footprint

5. Understand rating distribution patterns

Key Insights :

7,432 restaurants analyzed across multiple cities

2. Average rating of 2.66 indicates potential service quality gap

3. Barbeque Nation operates in 22 cities — strongest franchise presence

4. New Delhi is the highest restaurant density hub

5. Majority of restaurants fall under the "Cheap" category

6. Mid-tier ratings dominate, suggesting competitive but saturated market

Tools & Skills Used :

Tableau (Dashboard Design & Visualization)

2. Data Cleaning & Preparation

3. KPI Design

4. Exploratory Data Analysis

5. Business Insight Generation

Analytical Approach :

Aggregated ratings to identify top performers

2. Compared vote counts to measure customer engagement

3. Segmented restaurants by pricing tiers

4. Created dynamic Top-N filter for interactive exploration
